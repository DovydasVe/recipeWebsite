Pizza

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 scoops (60g) chocolate protein powder
  • 4 medium bananas (480g), sliced and frozen ahead of time
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/8 tsp peppermint or mint extract
  • 2 tbsp (15g) cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup (40g) chocolate chips

Place all ingredients in a large food processor or blender until mixture is light and fluffy. Line a bread tin with parchment paper, pour mixture in and smoothen out with a rubber spatula. Sprinkle on chocolate chips and then freeze for about one hour.

FACTS PER SERVING
Calories: 502 Protein: 29g Carbs: 74g Fat: 10g Makes 2 Servings
