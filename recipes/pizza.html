Pizza

INGREDIENTS

  • 6oz (170g) cooked chicken breast, cubed or shredded
  • 1 12‚Äù (30cm) whole wheat thin pizza crust
  • 1/8 cup (35g) spaghetti sauce
  • 1/4 cup (30g) low fat mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 3 mushrooms (45g), sliced
  • 1/4 cup onion (40g), chopped

Preheat oven to 425F/220C. Place pizza crust on a cookie sheet or other ovenproof pan and top with spaghetti sauce, chicken, mushrooms, onions and cheese. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until heated through.
